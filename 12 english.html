Q-1) “She was an Intresting girl” – Who said this, to whom and when ? What else did the passenger say ? What replay he get ? [3+1+2]


Ans – In Ruskin Bond’s short story ‘The Eye’s Have it’ The narrator say this to the passenger who got into the train at Saharanpur. When the new passenger remarked that he was nearly as attractive a travelling companion as the one who had just left. The narrator then asked about the hair of the girl and he wanted to know if whether she had kept her hair long or short. The new passenger replied that he had not noticed her hair but the girl had beautiful eyes. He also remark she had kept her hair long or short.
